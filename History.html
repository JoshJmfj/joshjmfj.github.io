History of the Pyramids

The Pyramids of Giza have a rich and storied history that spans thousands of years...

Early Dynastic Period:

The construction of the pyramids began during the Early Dynastic Period, with the first pyramid, the Step Pyramid of Djoser, built around 2630 BC by the architect Imhotep.
